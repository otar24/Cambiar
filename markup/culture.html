Culture of Empowerment

At Cambiar, the same attributes that drive our approach to investing also define our culture. We are entrepreneurial and forward thinking. We set a high bar for ourselves and for one another. And we continuously evolve while maintaining a steadfast commitment to the clients we serve.

Cambiar is a meritocracy, which means that every single member of our team has the opportunity to thrive in our vibrant, collaborative environment. Although Cambiar has grown significantly over the years, today managing $x in assets—an increase of x percent in x years—the principles we embrace and characteristics we value have not changed since we first opened our doors nearly five decades ago.

Our intellectual curiosity encourages us to initiate new projects and learn new skills along the way. This open-minded approach to work also helps us to develop professionally and build the satisfaction that comes with making a real impact.
