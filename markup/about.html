Who is Cambiar

At Cambiar, our intellectual curiosity and active, research-driven investment approach embolden us to think differently. It’s how we’ve set ourselves apart from the competition for 45 years.

We focus exclusively on one investment discipline: relative value. We search for out-of-favor companies with strong fundamentals and identifiable catalysts for change. The benefit for our clients? By investing with the courage of our convictions, we discover companies we believe will reward investors over time.

Independent

We're independent in every sense of the word. We’re 100% employee-owned and aligned with our clients’ success. We have an entrepreneurial and collaborative culture, and we’re dedicated to continuous improvement.

Unwavering Active Investors

As active investors, we’re benchmark agnostic. We capitalize on the market’s short-term dysfunction to find quality companies poised for long-term success. Our high active share—a measure of how much we differ from the benchmark index—reflects our commitment to discovering good companies at attractive prices.

Intellectually Curious and Research-Driven

We come to work each day with an open mind and a drive to always learn more. This intellectual curiosity forms our unique perspective, which we share with clients to offer new insight.
